WHY ISLAMORADA?

Sportfishing Capital of the World

Its vibrant outdoor lifestyle with abundant marine life and coral reefs, its proximity to Miami for convenient day trips or getaways, and its blend of island relaxation with a unique cultural history and community. The name "Islamorada" itself, meaning "purple island," comes from the Spanish explorers' admiration for the purple skies at sunset.
Islamorada is a village within the Florida Keys that encompasses parts of six islands and is renowned for its vibrant coral reefs, offering opportunities for snorkeling and scuba diving. Beyond its natural beauty, Islamorada is also known as the "Sportfishing Capital of the World".
