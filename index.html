WHY ISLAMORADA?

Sportfishing Capital of the World

Its vibrant outdoor lifestyle with abundant marine life and coral reefs, its proximity to Miami for convenient day trips or getaways, and its blend of island relaxation with a unique cultural history and community. The name "Islamorada" itself, meaning "purple island," comes from the Spanish explorers' admiration for the purple skies at sunset.
Islamorada is a village within the Florida Keys that encompasses parts of six islands and is renowned for its vibrant coral reefs, offering opportunities for snorkeling and scuba diving. Beyond its natural beauty, Islamorada is also known as the "Sportfishing Capital of the World."

TOP THINGS TO DO IN ISLAMORADA

THREE MUST VISIT SPOTS

Rain Barrel Village

Snap a selfie with Betsy the Lobster, then explore tropical gardens and browse studios filled with handmade crafts, art, beachwear, home goods, and Keys-inspired souvenirs.

Address:

86700 Overseas Hwy, Islamorada, FL 33036

What it's famous for

Rain Barrel Village is famous for being a vibrant open-air shopping destination that perfectly captures the quirky, laid-back vibe of the Florida Keys. Visitors love snapping a selfie with the iconic “Big Betsy” lobster sculpture before wandering through tropical gardens and exploring unique studios filled with handmade crafts, original artwork, resort wear, home goods, and Florida Keys-inspired souvenirs.

Learn more

Founders Park

Founders Park in Islamorada is a 45-acre community space with a beach, Olympic-size pool, tennis and pickleball courts, dog park, fitness trails, and picnic areas overlooking Florida Bay. The park hosts concerts, festivals, sports, and family activities year-round. It’s free for residents, with day passes available for visitors.

Address:

87000 Overseas Hwy, Islamorada, FL 33036

What it's famous for

Founders Park is known for its Olympic-size pool, splash pad, beach, tennis and pickleball courts, sports fields, and picnic areas. It also hosts community events and family activities along scenic Florida Bay.

Learn more

Marathon Bagel at Islamorada

Marathon Bagel Co. is famous for authentic New York-style bagels baked fresh daily, with a variety of sweet and savory cream cheeses. Visitors love their Nova Lox, Whitefish Salad, and Chorizo, all served in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere that brings a true taste of New York to the Florida Keys.

Address:

86701 Overseas Hwy, Islamorada, FL 33036

What it's famous for

Marathon Bagel Co. is known for authentic New York/New Jersey-style bagels, baked fresh daily with a variety of sweet and savory cream cheeses. They’re celebrated for high-quality ingredients. atmosphere, offering a true taste of New York in the Florida Keys.

About Florida Keys

The Florida Keys are a string of tropical islands stretching about 120 miles off the southern tip of Florida, between the Atlantic Ocean and Gulf of Mexico. They’re known for fishing, boating, snorkeling, and diving.
